Work hours can feel long. Study sessions even longer. You don't want the mental overhead of curating playlists or paying for a streaming subscription — you just want something good playing in the background.

That's exactly what radio is for.

## What is Mellow Bangla?

[Mellow Bangla](https://mellowbangla.com) is a 24/7 internet radio station playing Bengali, Hindi, and English romantic songs — handpicked and arranged so carefully that you'll rarely want to skip. The playlist runs around the clock with no ads, no interruptions, and no cost. Just press play and let it run.

## What is Radiola?

[Radiola](https://github.com/SokoloffA/radiola) is a free, open-source Mac app that lives quietly in your menu bar. It lets you search, save, and play internet radio stations with a single click — no browser tab needed, no fuss.

---

## Setting Up Mellow Bangla in 5 Minutes

### Step 1 — Download and Open Radiola

Install via Homebrew:

```bash
brew install --cask radiola
```

Or download the `.dmg` directly from the [Radiola GitHub releases page](https://github.com/SokoloffA/radiola/releases).

Once installed, open it from your Applications folder or Launchpad.

![Open Radiola from App Manager](/blog/mellow-bangla/1%20Open%20From%20AppManger.png)

### Step 2 — Find Radiola in the Menu Bar

Radiola appears as a small icon in your Mac's menu bar. Click it to open the panel.

![Locate Radiola in the menu bar](/blog/mellow-bangla/2%20Locate%20in%20Menubar.png)

### Step 3 — Open the Main Interface

Click the grid/browse icon inside the panel to open the full station browser.

![Open Radiola's main interface](/blog/mellow-bangla/3%20Open%20radiola's%20main%20interface.png)

### Step 4 — Search for Mellow Bangla

Use the **"By Name"** search tab and type **Mellow Bangla**. The station will appear in the results.

![Search for Mellow Bangla by name](/blog/mellow-bangla/4%20Select%20By%20Name%20and%20search%20Mellow%20Bangla.png)

### Step 5 — Mark It as a Favorite

Click the **home icon** next to Mellow Bangla to add it to your favorites.

![Turn on the home/favorite icon](/blog/mellow-bangla/5%20Turn%20home%20icon%20on.png)

### Step 6 — Confirm in Your Home List

Head to the **Home** tab to confirm Mellow Bangla is saved there.

![Mellow Bangla saved in favorites](/blog/mellow-bangla/6%20Go%20to%20home%20and%20mark%20mellow%20bangla%20as%20fav%20station.png)

### Step 7 — Play Directly from the Menu Bar

From now on, just click the Radiola icon in your menu bar, select Mellow Bangla, and hit play. That's it.

![Play Mellow Bangla directly from the menu bar](/blog/mellow-bangla/7%20Now%20you%20can%20find%20mellow%20bangla%20on%20menubar%20just%20click%20and%20play.png)
